About Us

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology. Everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs. We at GDG-BVP, use Google Technologies, in-order to produce some great solutions and applications. GDG-BVP was started as an initiative to promote the use of Google & other open Source technologies. We believe, we are a group of students who can code, design & use technology to innovate. We love to share our knowledge and simultaneously promoting the use of Google technologies.

Our Events

Some of our poplular events

APLLIED CS WITH ANDRIOD COURSE (10.02.2016 - 20.04.2016)

Applied computer science with android is a Google initiative to help university students understand and apply computer science concept using the android platform. The program had concept related to data structures and algorithms and artificial intelligence.


GOOGLE STUDY JAM(2016)- ANDROID FOR BEGINNERS (27.05.2016)

The aim was to create a group of developers, irrespective of their course departments and achieving certifications in a beginner level android course. Students gained interest and confidence after what they learn in the field of android development. The participants were confident enough to build simple android apps.

DEVELOPER’S FEST (31.10.2015)

A game development event by ‘GDG – BVP’ organized DEVELOPER’S FEST. Tech-savvy geeks were very excited and enrolled in the event to build their own games.



BIG DATA ANALYTICS- BY ARICENT (19.10.2015)

This event focused on the basics of big data. It briefly introduced the topic as what is it and why is it. It also explained the Hadoop echo system. Students were more interested in the role of No SQL Data base in big data analytics.
