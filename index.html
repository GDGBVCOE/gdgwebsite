About Us

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology. Everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs. We at GDG-BVP, use Google Technologies, in-order to produce some great solutions and applications. GDG-BVP was started as an initiative to promote the use of Google & other open Source technologies. We believe, we are a group of students who can code, design & use technology to innovate. We love to share our knowledge and simultaneous promote the use of Google technologies.

Our Events

The popular tech society ‘GDG – BVP’ started in 2011 reached its new heights of achievement in the session 2015-2016. The main motive of the establishment of the organization was not only to promote the Google technologies present and booming, but also to enhance the caliber of students with a hands-on experience with those technologies. The following events were effectively and efficiently conducted throughout the year.

APLLIED CS WITH ANDRIOD COURSE (10.09.2016 – 20.12.2016)

Applied computer science with android is a Google initiative to help university students understand and apply computer science concept using the android platform. The program had concept related to data structures and algorithms and artificial intelligence.


CODELAB ON PROGRESSIVE WEB APPS (18 October 2016)

This code lab was hands on session on creating your own Progressive Web App, including the design considerations, as well as implementation details to ensure that your app meets the key principles of a Progressive Web App. Progressive Web Apps are experiences that combine the best of the web and the best of apps.

GOOGLE DEVELOPER’S FEST (08.10.2016)

GDG New Delhi and GDG BVP together organized DevFest 16, the biggest Google tech conference in New Delhi. It had various sessions and lectures about Android, Web and Cloud from the experts.



GOOGLE I/O EXTENDED AND TECHNICAL SESSIONS (AT GOOGLE OFFICE)

I/O Extended events include a variety of options for developers—from live streaming sessions to local demos, to hackathons, codelabs, and more. Google Developer Groups New Delhi regularly organizes Google I/O sessions, and technical sessions with developers from the city. It includes Google I/O Extended Session, Codelabs and other activities. These events are regularly attended by members of GDG BVP.
